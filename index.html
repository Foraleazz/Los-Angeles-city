1. What city did I choose?

I chose Los Angeles. It is located in California, on the west coast of the United States. The city is famous for Hollywood, beaches and cultural diversity.

Population and Climate

Los Angeles has about 3.8 million inhabitants. The climate is Mediterranean: hot, dry summers and mild winters.

2. Background / History

Los Angeles was founded in 1781. The city grew quickly because of agriculture, the railway and later the movie industry. In the 20th century Hollywood made Los Angeles one of the most famous cities in the world.

3. Famous Places & Tourist Attractions

The center of the global movie industry.

4. Economy & Important Companies

Los Angeles has a strong economy. Important industries are:

  • Film industry
  • Technology
  • Tourism
  • Trade

Important companies and organizations include movie studios, technology companies and media companies.

5. Lifestyle & Culture

Los Angeles is known for its multicultural society. People from many different countries live there. The city offers:

  • Music
  • Art
  • Sports
  • Different types of food

6. Interesting Facts

  • Hollywood is the center of the American film industry.
  • Los Angeles hosted the Olympic Games in 1932 and 1984.
  • The city has more cars than many other American cities.
  • Los Angeles is one of the largest cities in the USA.

7. Problem: Homelessness

What is the problem?

Los Angeles has a serious homelessness problem. Many people cannot afford housing because rents are very high.

Data Example

Approximate number of homeless people:

Around 75,000 people experience homelessness in Los Angeles County.

What does the data show?

The data shows that homelessness is one of the biggest social challenges in Los Angeles. High housing prices and lack of affordable homes are important reasons.
